About MECHCONNECT

The Digital Hub for Mechanical Engineering Students at FUTO

What is MECHCONNECT?

MECHCONNECT is the official online platform designed for Mechanical Engineering students at the Federal University of Technology Owerri (FUTO). It connects all levels from 100L to 500L, including lecturers and departmental administrators, making academic and engineering collaboration easier.

The platform serves as a digital home for communication, learning, engineering projects, research, skill development, and departmental updates. It is built to solve the long-time disconnect between levels and to help students grow academically, socially, and professionally.

Core Features of MECHCONNECT

📚 Lecture Notes & Resources

Access past questions, lecture materials, tutorial videos, and departmental documents.

🧠 Academic Community

Connect with students across levels. Share ideas, ask questions, and discuss courses.

🔧 Engineering Projects Hub

Showcase final-year projects, 300L designs, and departmental innovations.

📢 Departmental Updates

Stay updated with announcements: timetables, test dates, events, SIWES info.

🛒 Marketplace

Buy and sell textbooks, drawing materials, calculators, services, and tools.

💡 Skill Development

Learn web development, Fusion 360, Python, ANSYS, CAD/CAE, machining, and more.

🧾 MCJ-Attendance Integration

Smart attendance tracking for courses, with biometric verification support.

☁ Cloud-Connected System

All data backed up online with secure and scalable cloud architecture.

The Mission

The mission of MECHCONNECT is to create a modern engineering environment where learning, collaboration, and innovation thrive. The platform aims to:

  • Improve communication across all levels
  • Make engineering resources accessible to every student
  • Showcase engineering work done in the department
  • Encourage students to build real-world technical projects
  • Connect Mechanical Engineering FUTO with the global digital world

Who Built MECHCONNECT?

MECHCONNECT is part of the larger MCJtech ecosystem created by Morkah Chiemerie Joshua (MCJ), a Mechanical Engineering student at FUTO.

MCJtech focuses on engineering systems, software tools, attendance automation, Maglev research, and digital platforms designed to bring engineering education closer to students.
